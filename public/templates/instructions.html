How to play

This game requires a computer,
and at least two mobile devices to play.

  • 1. create a new game on a computer
  • 2. turn your mobile device sideways
  • 3. on your mobile device, navigate to the game url, provided by the new game
  • 4. fill out your name
  • 5. wait until all players are ready
  • 6. use your mobile device as a stearing wheel to control your kurve
  • 7. don't collide with borders or trails left by the kurves
  • 8. the last kurve alive wins
